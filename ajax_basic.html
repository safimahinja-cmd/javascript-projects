AJAX Assignment

XMLHttpRequest demo — click the button to load content without reloading the page.

How it works

  • An XMLHttpRequest object is created.
  • The request is opened with open() and sent with send().
  • When readyState reaches 4 and status is 200, the response is displayed.
  • The page updates dynamically — no full reload needed.

Content will appear here after you click the button.
